histomicstkÂ¶

This histomicstk package contains algorithms for fundamental image analysis tasks including color normalization, color deconvolution, filtering to enhance commonly found objects/structures, cell/nuclei segmentation, feature extraction, and tissue detection. It also contains algorithms and workflows to parse annotations polygons into masks for training and validating algorithms and vice versa.
